WE TEACH

Our classes are conducted all 7 days a week in the morning or evening in Janakpuri park, New Delhi.

WE STRENGTHEN

Due to the age differences, we conduct adaptive classes where each faculty member teaches a few students based on his/her level.

WE PLAY

On Sundays, we conduct an activity day where all students play sports like kho-kho, football, cricket, bowling, etc. which help them enjoy and learn.

WE PROVIDE FOOD

We serve healthy meals and refreshments to the students on a regular basis. Many a time we also provide lunch during classes.

WE ENJOY FESTIVALS

We conduct special events for every festival, be it Diwali, Holi, Dussehra, Gurupurab, Gandhi Jayanti and many more.

WE HAVE FUN

We want that students should learn while having fun. Hence we conduct educational picnics for studentsâ€™ learning and enjoyment.
